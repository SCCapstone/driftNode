Instructions for Setting Up the Camera

Open up the terminal.
Enter the command: sudo apt-get update
Wait until it finishes.
Enter the comand: sudo apt-get upgrade
Wait until it finishes.
Enter the comand: sudo rpi-update
Wait until it finishes.
Enter the comand: sudo raspi-config
Navigate to the "camera" option, and enable it. Select finish and reboot the pi.
To test it, enter the command: raspistill -o test.jpg
If it worked a window will show up on the desktop with what the camera sees. A red LED will turn on (on the RPi camera).
